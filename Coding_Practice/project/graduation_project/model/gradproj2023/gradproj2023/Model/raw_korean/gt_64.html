20년 후 — Mary Kay Letourneau는 이전 학생인 Vili Fualaau와 여전히 (기술적으로) 함께 있습니다.

업데이트 날짜: 2018년 5월 29일 오전 10:17
안나 퀸 타나

인스 타 그램

학생과 성관계를 맺은 혐의로 유죄 판결을 받은 지 20년이 지난 메리 케이 레투르노(Mary Kay Letourneau) 와 빌리 푸알라우( Vili Fualaau )는 논란이 되고 있는 그들의 관계에 대해 더 많은 세부 사항을 공유할 준비가 되어 있습니다. 5월 29일 오늘 방송되는 2시간 분량의 A&E 다큐멘터리에서 Mary Kay는 감옥 이후 자신의 삶을 공개하고 시청자에게 현재 자신의 삶에 대한 내면을 보여줍니다.

1997년 당시 34세의 6학년 교사였던 메리 케이(Mary Kay)는 그녀의 남편 스티브 레투르노(Steve Letourneau)가 자신이 12세 학생인 빌리(Vili)와 성관계를 갖고 있다는 사실을 알게 된 후 아동 성폭행 혐의로 체포되어 기소되었습니다. 재판이 끝나기를 기다리는 동안 그녀는 부부의 큰 딸인 오드리에게 출산을 했습니다. 얼마 지나지 않아 그녀는 유죄 판결을 받아 징역 6개월과 성범죄자 치료 3년을 선고받았습니다.

그러나 석방된 지 2주 후, 그녀는 자신의 차에서 빌리와 성관계를 가진 것이 적발되어 7년 6개월의 징역형을 선고 받았습니다. 그녀는 또 다시 임신을 하여 딸 조지아를 낳았습니다. Mary Kay와 Vili는 그들의 경험을 담은 Only One Crime, Love 라는 책을 썼으며 같은 해 프랑스에서 출판되었습니다.

(사진 제공: 스플래쉬)

메리 케이는 2004년 석방됐고 1년 뒤 당시 법적 성년이 된 빌리와 결혼했다. 2015년 바바라 월터스(Barbara Walters) 와의 인터뷰에서 메리 케이(Mary Kay)가 감옥에 있는 동안 전 학생과 소통할 수 있는 창의적인 방법을 찾았다는 사실이 밝혀졌습니다 . "Mary는 다양한 방법으로 Vili와 연락을 유지했습니다. 한 가지 방법은 그녀가 감옥에서 아기들을 위해 우유를 생산하고 있다는 메시지를 우유병에 ​​담아 보내는 것이었습니다. 그래서 그녀는 모자에 작은 메모를 적었고 그들은 갔다. "라고 사례 전문가 Gregg Olsen이 설명했습니다. "그녀는 오디오 테이프를 보냈습니다. 그녀는 [Vili]에게 직접 연락할 수 없었기 때문에 수감자들에게 전화를 걸도록 했습니다."

그러나 56세의 메리 케이(Mary Kay)와 34세의 빌리(Vili)는 평생 뒤따른 스캔들에도 불구하고 계속해서 딸들을 함께 키우고 있습니다. 오늘 불명예스러운 교사에 대한 업데이트를 보려면 아래를 계속 읽으십시오 .

Mary Kay Letourneau는 아직 결혼했습니까?

(사진 제공: 게티 이미지)

Technically, yes — but Vili filed for separation from his former middle school teacher last summer. Shortly after, Mary Kay filed her own paperwork to have the case dismissed — prompting Vili to confess that it’s all "a publicity stunt." Vili explained his rationale for separating, claiming he could not move forward with his “Cigaweed” business because he was married to a convict.

"There is a piece of paper that is filed but that doesn’t depict what is going on,” he told Radar Online at the time. "Be patient. It’s not serious as people think it is... Maybe we’re sleeping in different rooms. The paperwork that is filed doesn’t have all that information."

What does Mary Kay Letourneau do for a living?

Currently, Mary Kay does not have a job — and is supported by her husband, who works as a DJ (Mary Kay has been spotted at his "hot teacher" gigs and signs autographs for guests) and he is trying to get into the "Cigaweed" business in Washington where they now live. However, in 2015, Mary Kay revealed she wants to be taken off the sex offender registry and be allowed to teach again. "Recently I said, 'It's been 10 years, why don't I lift that?'" she told Barbara Walters. "There's a process, there's a form, you take it to court and then they grant it if it looks like it should be granted." However, that doesn't seem to have happened yet.

(Photo Credit: Splash)

What are Mary Kay Letourneau's children doing today?

Before her marriage to Vili, Mary Kay had four children —Steve Jr., 34, Mary Claire, 31, Nicholas, 27, and Jacqueline, 25 — with her first husband Steve. Today, Nicholas is a graduate of Georgetown University, where he studied computer engineering and Steve, who is two years younger than Vili, is a father-of-two. Very little is known about her oldest daughters, but Mary Kay insists all her kids are extremely close. However, Vili has admitted in the past that it is weird having stepchildren close to his age. "It’s an awkward feeling, for sure, to be close in age with someone [who is] technically your stepson or stepdaughter," he said in the 2015 interview.

Mary Kay's daughters with Vili are also all grown up — and beautiful. Audrey, 20, and Georgia, 19, revealed they have never had a sit-down chat with their parents about their controversial relationship but know all about it. Their dad also revealed how he warned them to not date at a young age "The reason for me telling them [not to have boyfriends] was just from, out of experience,” Vili explained. “A relationship could lead to something that you think you wanted back then. You don’t really want it, maybe, years later.” When asked about teacher/student relationships, he added, "I don’t support younger kids being married or having a relationship with someone older. I don’t support it."

Mary Kay Letourneau's first husband has moved on, too!

(Photo Credit: ABC News)

Following her arrest in 1997, Steve filed for divorce and got full custody of the couple's four kids. Steve has worked for Alaskan Airlines for over 30 years and he has a daughter by his second ex-wife, Kelly Whalen. An insider revealed he is planning to marry his current longtime girlfriend. "He has little communication with Mary Kay,” a source told Radar Online. “But he’s gotten on with his life and everything is positive. He is a wonderful man and father. None of the kids have turned out bad and he did it all on his own. Steven is a wonderful, hardworking man. He’s always provided and care for his family."

And he proved it. When asked about Mary Kay and Vili's separation last year, he told People magazine, "I’ve moved on and I have nothing to say."
