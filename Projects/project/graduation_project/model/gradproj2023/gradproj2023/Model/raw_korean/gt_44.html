EPIC은 곧 Fortnite의 새로운 시즌을 시작할 예정이며 예상대로 이미 개발자가 이상한 새로운 것을 괴롭히는 것을 보고 있습니다. 지금까지 시즌 6에 대해 우리가 알고 있는 모든 것은 다음과 같습니다.

포트나이트 시즌 6 - 다음에는 어떻게 되나요?

우리는 Fortnite 시즌 6에 대한 첫 번째 구체적인 티저를 제공하는 Epic의 새로운 업데이트를 방금 보았습니다. 

"모든 멋진 파티에는 DJ가 필요합니다."라는 트윗에는 사이버 DJ처럼 옷을 입은 라마의 사진이 포함되어 있습니다(Deadmau5/Daft Punk를 생각해 보세요).

트윗은 "시즌 6까지 3일 남았습니다"라고 마무리됩니다.

힌트입니다. 파티 테마나 음악 테마를 구하고 있나요? 호수가 거대한 파티 화산으로 변할까요?

필요한 모든 정보를 얻으려면 Fortnite 시즌 6 카운트다운을 확인하세요 .

Fortnite 시즌 6이 곧 시작될 예정이며, 시즌 5에서 시즌 6으로의 변경을 앞두고 Epic이 새로운 콘텐츠를 공개할 것으로 기대됩니다.

시즌 4/5 크로스오버에서 하늘에 거대한 균열이 열린 것을 본 반면, 5/6 기간에는 거대하고 신비한 큐브가 맵에 들어와 저절로 움직이는 것을 보았습니다. 

여기저기서 열리는 다른 균열과 번개로 플레이어에게 피해를 주는 다른 균열 사이에서 게임 내에서 더 많은 시즌 6 변경 사항이 발생하는 것을 볼 수 있는 과정이 진행되고 있다고 말하는 것이 안전합니다. 

다음은 Fortnite 시즌 6의 모든 것에 대한 전체 가이드입니다. 더 많은 정보가 공개되면 업데이트하겠습니다. 

DS

(Pic: DS)

포트나이트 시즌 6 - 언제 시작되나요?

Epic은 아직 시즌 6의 시작 날짜를 공식적으로 발표하지 않았습니다.

However, it appears that thanks to the length of Seasons so far, we can expect Season 6 to begin on September 20. We can extrapolate that information based on what we know about the length of Seasons (Season 5 started on July 12, and they tend to last about two months) 

Epic is usually a little bit flexible with the dates new Seasons start on though, thanks to the work that goes into the backend of the game when the changeovers occur.

So depending on the weight of the work the developers need to put in, we could be seeing more (or less!) of a wait time.

    Fortnite Season 6 - What's with the Cube?

    As of last month, a giant purple Cube entered Fortnite and has been rolling around the map, confusing players as it goes. The bizarre item surely holds the clues to what's coming next in the game...

    To understand the Cube, you need to understand Fortnite 'lore' so far. It began when a missile launched and tore a rift open in the sky. Then, from the rifts, new areas came to the game and a giant temple celebrating a tomato-based God appeared, too. The Enforcer skin dropped, which hinted at putting a stop to these space/time shenanigans, and the rift began to close. 

    But as it closed, it summoned a sentient Cube. The Cube - which punishes those that shoot at it with deadly bolts of purple lightning - moves every two hours towards an undisclosed destination. Datamined information suggests the Cube is en route to Loot Lake, but as of right now, its motivations and destination remain a mystery. 

    The Cube made its way to Loot Lake and melted, creating a big bouncy lake - and that's where we're at now.

    DS

    (Pic: DS)

    Fortnite Season 6 - What is the theme?

    We haven't been told what's going on with Season 6 just yet, but there are a few clues.

    In a recent tease for the Capoeira emote, Epic seemed to let slip that a castle will be joining the game (you can just about see it in the background, very far away). This, paired with the 'Crazy Castle' spray introduced in the game as part of the Season 5 Battle Pass, suggests we may be getting a fantasy/Medieval theme.

    That would continue the time-travel narrative Epic has introduced so far - maybe the Cube will zap the map back into the past? 

    Who knows at this point... Epic likes to tease and keep players on their toes, so we're excited to see what comes next.

    Fortnite Season 6 - What Skins will be introduced?

    Every new Season sees the introduction of new skins. At the time of writing, we've not had any leaks or datamines that reveal what sort of skins we'll be seeing as of the start of Season 6.

    Maybe if we do go back in time, we can expect to see Medieval knights and dragon-themed skins. Maybe we can see mysterious Mayan or Aztec skins. Maybe Neanderthals. Who knows?

    Perhaps the space/time Enforcer thing will be explored more, too - we'll update our gallery and include new information in this page as more infomation presents itself.

      Fortnite Season 6 - Hats

      Rumours abound that Fortnite will be getting hats soon, too. A set of leaks taken from datamined files of the newest build of the game suggests makeovers to older costumes could be coming to the game, supported by new custom headwear.

      Data suggested that ???favorite_hat,??? ???favorite_vehicledeco,??? and ???favorite_mapmarker??? would be introduced to the game. 

      Coming off the back of the Tomato head that was introduced to the game recently, it would make sense to see more custom head items introduced - and for Epic it makes sense: more items to unlock means more rewards for players, and another revenue stream introduced to the game, too.

      DS

      (Pic: DS)

      Fortnite Season 6 - Custom Battle Bus

      In the datamine for patch 5.40, players have managed to determine that different Battle Buses will be coming to the game.

      Thanks to a text field in the title, we learned Fortnite will:

      "Randomly chooses between favorite Battle Buses (or all owned Battle Buses if nothing is marked as a favorite)"

      One example we've seen so far is a Purple Battle bus (which we know about thanks to game files) though we've not seen it yet.

      DS

      (Pic: DS)

      Fortnite Season 6 - Weapon Skins

      The same patch revealed that we'll see a large number of high profile weapons get custom skin options, too.

      This is something that's been requested heavily by the Fortnite community and is a great addition to the game - despite having a third-person view that obscures your weapon from your own view most of the time.

      No skin examples have been leaked just yet, but we're expecting to hear more about them soon.

      DS

      (Pic: DS)

      Fortnite Season 6 - New Consumables

      Part of the version 5.40 datamine has revealed a new consumable items called 'Balloons'.

      From the text supporting the item, it appears the consumable helps players gracefully fall, float and possibly fly - similar to the Hop Rocks item we've seen before.

      'Rapid inflate balloons to help break your fall or soar to new heights!' reads the item description.

      We're intrigued to see how this is going to work in-game.
